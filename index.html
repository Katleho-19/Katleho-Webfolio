About Me

I'm an IT student at the Central University of Technology in Welkom, Free State. I run a small internet café at home and enjoy turning ideas into responsive websites.

I've worked with C# and SQL to build data-driven applications, and I'm currently exploring Dart and Flutter to expand into mobile app development.

I love learning new technologies and building projects that solve real problems. Outside of programming I enjoy gaming, music and photography, and love to collaborate with others.

As a motivated student and C# developer I continuously seek opportunities to learn and adapt to new technologies fluent in HTML5, CSS, SQL, Dart and C#, I bring a creative mind and a passion for innovative problem solving.
